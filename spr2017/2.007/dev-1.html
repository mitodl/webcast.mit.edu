This year’s competition theme is "May the torque be with you". Using a universal kit of tools and materials, students have been challenged to build a robot that can navigate a game board resembling the Starwars X-wing fighter. They will each develop their own strategy for gaining points, and the student whose robot earns the most points at the end of each duel will progress to the next round until there is only one champion left standing.
