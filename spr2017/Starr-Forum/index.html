SystemsThatLearn@CSAIL Initiative Launch

March 29, 2017 | 12:00 p.m. EST
Room 32-155, First Floor
Ray and Maria Stata Center

We invite you to attend the launch of the SystemsThatLearn@CSAIL initiative. The goal of SystemsThatLearn@CSAIL is to accelerate the development, deployment, and evolution of large scale software systems that incorporate Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

Join STL@CSAIL faculty directors Sam Madden and Tommi Jaakkola, along with founding industry members for the launch of this exciting new collaboration.
