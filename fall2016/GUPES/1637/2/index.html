    GUPES Green Gown Awards Ceremony

    The GUPES Green Gown Awards winners will be announced at the Awards Ceremony which will be held on Tuesday 13th September 2016 in Boston, USA.
    The GUPES Green Gown Awards Ceremony will take place after the GUPES Steering Group meeting (invitation only) between 5pm – 7pm EST.
    The GUPES Green Gown Awards Ceremony will take place prior to the 3rd World Symposium on Sustainable Development at Universities at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Further details on the 3rd World Symposium can be found at https://sustainability.mit.edu/wssd2016. You do not have to attend the full event to attend the Awards Ceremony.

     
    It is free to attend the GUPES Green Gown Awards Ceremony. All travel and subsistence expenses are at the institution/attendee’s own cost.
     

    Alternatively, you can also attend the Awards Ceremony virtually. The GUPES Green Gown Awards Ceremony will be filmed and will be publically available to watch through webstream. Details will be sent on how to watch prior to the Awards Ceremony.
    After the Awards Ceremony a press release will be issued announcing the winners and this will be sent to all Finalists and will be available on the website at www.greengownawards.org. 


    How to attend the GUPES Green Gown Awards Ceremony
    Places at the Awards Ceremony are limited so if you are attending the Awards Ceremony please complete the below form and return this togreengown@eauc.org.uk by Friday 9th September at the latest. Further details on the location of the Awards Ceremony will be sent to you. Please complete for each person attending.
