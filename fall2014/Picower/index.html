About
Distinguished neuroscientists from renowned global academic and medical institutions will gather on October 21, 2014 at the Picower Institute for Learning and Memory to present their most recent findings associated with brain circuitry. Symposium presenters will highlight conclusions from the latest research related to the role neural circuits play in behavior and cognition. The panel will share insights regarding how the brain’s wiring system impacts and regulates everything from fear and anxiety to spatial perception, vision, sensory responses, decision making, sleep patterns, learning, and memory.
