    On Thursday May 11th from 10am-11:30am EDT (-4 UTC), the u.lab 2x and Just Money teams will host a joint Live Session on "The Future of Finance and Climate Initiatives".

    Tune in to hear from a number of leaders in the field, including:

    Christiana Figueres - Executive Secretary of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC) 2010-2016. Christiana led the successful negotiation for the historic Paris Agreement of 2015.

    Peter Blom - CEO of Triodos Bank in Europe. Triodos Bank has been a leader in the sustainable banking movement and is considered one of the most environmentally sustainable banks in the world.

     
    Peter Senge - Senior Lecturer in Leadership and Sustainability at the MIT Sloan School of Management and author of the widely acclaimed book, The Fifth Discipline: The Art and Practice of The Learning Organization (1990).
