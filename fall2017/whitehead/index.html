WHITEHEAD COLLOQUIUM

The Whitehead Colloquium, held each fall at the Institute in Cambridge, is the signature event for members of the Board of Associates (BOA) and the Cambridge community. The event features keynote addresses by our faculty and first-hand updates on the Institute’s latest biological breakthroughs.
