    FACULTY FORUM ONLINE: DEAN IAN WAITZ

     

     


    Ian A. Waitz is Dean of the School of Engineering and the Jerome C. Hunsaker Professor of Aeronautics and Astronautics at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He has been on the faculty at MIT since 1991 and was the department head in Aeronautics and Astronautics from 2008 until his appointment as dean in 2011.


    As Dean, Waitz has focused on advancing the mission of the School of Engineering through the development of new programs and spaces for innovation and entrepreneurship, novel models and opportunities in residential education, expanded pathways for engagement with the Institute's alumni, friends, and industry partners, and programs and policies that will further enable MIT's ability to provide an exceptional learning and research environment for students, faculty, and staff...[read more]
